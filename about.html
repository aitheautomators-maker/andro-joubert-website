The Maker

The story of a young South African knifemaker turning passion into precision.

the story

Andro Joubert

Andro Joubert is a young, ambitious knifemaker from South Africa who made the bold decision to turn his lifelong passion into a full-time craft. On December 1st, 2024, he officially committed to making knives professionally, and has not looked back since.

His journey did not happen overnight. Andro trained under the guidance of Jaco de Kock, one of South Africa's most respected custom knifemakers. In Jaco's shop, Andro learned the fundamentals of precision blade work, from hollow grinding to the intricate mechanics of framelock and linerlock folding knives.

Every day brings something new: a new technique to master, a new material to explore, a new design to push forward. Andro puts his heart and soul into every detail of his knives, ensuring each one that leaves his workshop meets his exacting standards.

His brother works on a farm in the USA and regularly takes a bagful of Andro's knives across the Atlantic. This connection has quietly given Andro's work an international presence, with his blades finding homes well beyond South African borders.

the journey

Knife Making Timeline

4 YEARS

Working in the USA

Andro spent approximately 4 years working on a farm in Arkansas, USA. This experience broadened his perspective and deepened his appreciation for quality tools, fuel that would later feed his knifemaking fire.

2019-2023

Learning the Craft

Through years of dedicated practice and mentorship under Jaco de Kock, Andro developed his skills, from basic fixed blades to complex titanium framelock mechanisms with precision-fit carbon fiber components.

DEC 2024

Goes Full-Time

On December 1st, 2024, Andro made the life-changing decision to pursue knifemaking as his full-time profession. What started as a dream became a daily reality, and he has never been happier.

2025+

Building a Name

Now a recognized name among South Africa's custom knife community, Andro continues to refine his craft, release new models, and build a reputation for quality that reaches international collectors.

principles

Craft Philosophy

Obsessive Detail

Every edge, every fit, every finish is scrutinized. Nothing leaves the workshop unless it meets Andro's exacting standards.

Continuous Growth

Every knife is a learning opportunity. Andro's skills evolve with each project. The next is always better than the last.

Truly Handmade

No CNC shortcuts. Every blade is shaped, ground, and finished by hand. Each piece carries the mark of its maker.

Open Communication

Andro works closely with collectors. Your Request is a collaboration, your vision brought to life through his hands.
