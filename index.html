Andro Joubert

Andro is a young, passionate knifemaker who turned his dream into a full-time reality on December 1st, 2024. Trained under the renowned Jaco de Kock, Andro brings a rare dedication to every single blade that leaves his workshop.

Every knife is an exercise in perfection — from the hollow grind of the blade to the precise fit of a titanium framelock. Andro pours his heart and soul into each detail, ensuring every piece is both a functional tool and a work of art.
